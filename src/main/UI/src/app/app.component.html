Essential Info

Arrival Information

  • Check-in: 3:00 PM
  • Check-out: 11:00 AM
  • Parking: Self-parking in the underground garage is CA$15 per day and valet-parking is CA$50 per day.
  • Airport Shuttle: Complimentary airport shuttles leave hourly for London International Airport. Shuttles to Toronto Pearson are available twice daily for CA$50.
  • Trains: The nearest station is London Station, with connections to Toronto, Sarnia, and Windsor.
  • Pet Policy: Pets of all sizes are allowed in designated areas. Service animals are welcome everywhere.

Landon Green Program

The Landon Hotel - London was recently renovated with sustainability in mind — green materials, solar power, and energy-friendly lighting throughout. Recycling and composting programs help reduce waste and support local gardens.

Dining in the Area

London, Ontario is a foodie’s paradise, with English, Italian, Indian, American, Chinese, and French cuisines within two blocks of the hotel.

Rooftop Café

Our Rooftop Café offers five-star dining and panoramic views. Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options available.

Smoothie Bar

Enjoy locally sourced smoothies while overlooking the city. Seasonal recipes and rotating selections available.

Breakfast & Coffee Bar

Start your day with fresh fruit, croissants, artisan bread, and coffee or tea. Breakfast served daily 7:00–10:00 AM.

Room Service

Prefer a quiet evening in? Room service is available for all dining options.
